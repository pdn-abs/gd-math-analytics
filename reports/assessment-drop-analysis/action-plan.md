## Global Gamification & Drops Enhancements
> A generalized action plan targeting the game-wide gamification (Drops system) introduced in Q4 2025. Instead of micro-managing drops per level, these global rules should be applied dynamically based on the cognitive profile of the level type.

### 1. Levels with "Initial Mechanics Understanding Difficulty" (Introductions / New Layouts)
* **Sensory Gating:** Delay all random/ambient drop spawns until the player has successfully completed the first guided "ghost-hand" step. Drops spanning immediately on load distract from learning new UI interactions.
* **Validation Bursts:** Trigger a massive, specific "ConfettiBlast" exactly when the player successfully performs the new interaction for the first time to strictly validate their comprehension.

### 2. Levels with "Solving the problem is difficult" (High Cognitive Load / Complex Math)
* **Effort vs. Speed:** Shift gamification away from speed. Use high-value 'Golden Coins' or specific 'ColorSplash' visual effects exclusively on first-try successes to discourage trial-and-error brute-forcing.
* **Anti-Anxiety Mechanics:** Trigger 'FreezeTimer' or 'SlowDown' drops when the user pauses for more than 5 seconds. This reduces anxiety and gamifies "taking time to think."
* **Tool Reinforcement:** If a level involves complex UI tools (like a chart ruler or isolating a matrix cell), spawn drops directly indicating the correct usage of the helper tool, incentivizing analytical approaches over blind guessing.

### 3. Levels with "Boring / Low Engagement" (High UI Friction / Repetitive Drilling)
* **Momentum Scaffolding:** Implement "Speed Combo" multipliers for rapid consecutive successes (e.g., matching shapes quickly) to inject a sense of urgency and battle repetitiveness.
* **Tactile Micro-Rewards:** For levels with heavy dragging manipulation (like counting out tens-blocks or filling shapes), play immediate tactile "Sparkle" effects purely on a valid drop-zone snap. Make the physical interaction itself feel satisfying.
* **Completion Pacing:** On deeply tedious drill boards, remove per-interaction drops so they don't interrupt flow. Instead, stockpile the value and award a massive "TreasureChest" or "MultiCoinPouch" only upon flawless board-clears.
